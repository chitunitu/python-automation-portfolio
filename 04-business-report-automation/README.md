# 04 - Automated Business Report Generator (Excel → Chart → PDF)

This system takes raw Excel business data and automatically generates:
- Sales analysis
- Visualization charts
- A professional PDF business report

✅ Skills Used:
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- PDF Automation
- Business Reporting
- Data Visualization

✅ Business Use:
- Automatic financial reporting
- Sales performance reports
- Management dashboards
- Business analytics

✅ Input:
- sales_data.xlsx

✅ Output:
- sales_chart.png
- final_sales_report.pdf
